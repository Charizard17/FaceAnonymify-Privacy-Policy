Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 03, 2023

Introduction

Welcome to FaceAnonymify! This app is provided by Esad Dursun and is intended for general use. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to ensuring that your personal information is treated with care. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

FaceAnonymify does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not collect, store, or share any data that can be used to identify you.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we have no data to use or share. The app's functionality is based on processing images that you provide from your device. We do not store or transmit any of these images to our servers.

Face Detection

FaceAnonymify uses the AWS - Amazon Rekognition service for face detection. This service allows us to detect faces in the images you provide. However, it is important to note that we do not store any images, and the face detection is performed locally on your device.

Links to Other Sites

Our app may contain links to external websites or services. Please note that we do not have control over these external sites and their privacy practices. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these websites before using them.

Children's Privacy

FaceAnonymify is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
